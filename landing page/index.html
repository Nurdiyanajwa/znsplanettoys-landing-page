POPULAR

Stuffed toys

A stuffed toy is a toy doll with an outer fabric sewn from a textile and stuffed with flexible material.They are known by many names such as plush toys, plushies, stuffed animals and stuffies.They may also be called soft toys or cuddly toys.

Lego

Lego is a line of plastic construction toys that are manufactured by The Lego Group. Lego pieces can be assembled and connected in many ways to construct objects, including vehicles and working robots.

Toy blocks

Toy blocks (also building bricks, building blocks, or simply blocks) are wooden, plastic, or foam pieces of various shapes and colors that are used as construction toys.Sometimes, toy blocks depict letters of the alphabet.
